2020 TRENDS

MINIMALIST MAGIC…

For understated glamour, a neat bouquet of roses is becoming a top trend for this year. Clean and crisp pure white roses with long stems create a pristine aesthetic. But, we're totally in love with the antique faded hue of quicksand roses - a hugely sought after choice.

SOFT AND DREAMY…

We adore this combination of fluffy pampas crass, cotton stems and giant protea for this neutral-toned floral wonder. A bouquet that incorporates long grasses, dried berries, and so many soft textures would look like pure magic flowing in the breeze down the aisle at an outdoor wedding. Cotton may not seem like an abnormal choice, but when it looks this good how could anyone say no!
